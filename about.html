Discover Who I Am

Ever wondered what happens when you mix tech, creativity, and a dash of organized chaos? Well, you're about to find out! Welcome to my world—where problem-solving meets passion, and every line of code tells a story.

My Present

I'm a front-end developer, problem solver, and lifelong experimenter. I love turning complex ideas into simple, functional, and beautiful digital experiences. Code is my current playground, but my curiosity extends far beyond the screen. I thrive in spaces where creativity meets logic, and I'm always learning, iterating, and refining—whether it's a website, a piece of furniture, or an urban garden.

I don't believe in a single "right path"—I believe in experiments. Web development is one of them: it challenges me to find simple solutions to complex problems while allowing me to create, connect, communicate, and constantly learn. Right now, I'm building my portfolio and exploring where this journey takes me.

My Past

Before diving into front-end development, I explored the worlds of graphic design, event management, translation, and hospitality—wearing many hats (sometimes literally). The common thread? Connection: between people, ideas and creativity. These experiences shaped my approach to problem-solving, balancing structure and flexibility, aesthetics and function.

Living in different countries and working across industries taught me adaptability. I've built furniture, organized events, grown an urban garden, volunteered internationally, taken on countless creative projects like sewing, painting, and vlogging. Each experience reinforced that growth isn't linear—it's an intricate web of experiments, failures, and discoveries.

My Future

To me, technology is a tool, not a goal. In the near future, I aim to work with companies and startups to grow my skills and learn from dynamic teams. But my long-term vision is freedom: working as a freelancer, traveling, and eventually building something of my own.

I dream of spaces that bring people and ideas together—coworking hubs, coliving spaces, urban gardens, and community-driven projects that promote sustainability, creativity and growth. For now, I'm learning, experimenting, expanding my portfolio and exploring new ways to share ideas—maybe even through a YouTube channel. My journey is still unfolding, and I'm open to wherever it leads.

My Values & Passions

Curiosity, authenticity, and a passion for lifelong learning drive everything I do. But more specifically:

Sustainability - I build things—whether websites or furniture—that are meant to last, with as little waste as possible.

Community - Everything is better when shared. I want my work to connect and empower people.

Problem-Solving - There's nothing I love more than breaking down complex challenges into simple, elegant solutions (bonus points if they make life easier for someone).

Beyond the code, you'll find me experimenting with DIY projects, growing plants on my terrace, learning about regenerative agriculture, and planning my next adventure.

Want to Connect?

I'm always open to new opportunities and collaborations. Whether you're looking for a front-end developer, a creative mind, or just someone to brainstorm with over coffee, let's chat!
